## Overview

These skill courses are offered to vocationalise UG and PG level by integrating domain-based learning in every programme offered by the University. There is provision for vertical mobility of skill level based on the demand.

**Course Structure:** All courses carry 4 credits with structure: 0+3+1 (Theory + Practice + Project) OR 0+2+2
