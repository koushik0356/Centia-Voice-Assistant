## 1. OVERVIEW

Centurion University has embraced the framework of **Skill Integrated Higher Education** since the enactment of the CUTM Act 2010. The university has established several **Centers of Excellence (CoEs)** through synergy between industry and academia.

---

## 2. EDUCATIONAL MODEL

### Integrated Educational Learning Model
CUTM operates through two main learning frameworks:
- **Action Learning Labs**
- **Production Centers**

### Learning Philosophy: "Learning by Doing"

The university's model fosters practical learning through:
- Hands-on experiences that drive innovation and social impact
- Real-world projects in Action Learning Labs and Production Centers
- Engagement in actual manufacturing and service production
- Enhanced employability through practical skill development
- Community engagement and sustainable development focus

### Key Benefits of CUTM's Model
- Empowers students through hands-on experiences
- Fosters innovation and social impact
- Addresses community needs while learning
- Integrates community stakeholders into education
- Ensures education actively contributes to sustainable development and societal progress
