# B.Tech in Electronics and Communication Engineering (ECE)
## Centurion University of Technology and Management, Andhra Pradesh

---

## Program Overview

**B.Tech in Electronics and Communication Engineering (ECE)** is a **four-year undergraduate program** that focuses on **designing, developing, and managing electronic devices, circuits, communication systems, and embedded technologies**. This program equips students with a **strong foundation in electronics, signal processing, telecommunications, embedded systems, VLSI design, and modern wireless communication technologies like 5G and IoT**.

With the growing demand for **smart devices, high-speed communication, and automation**, ECE graduates play a crucial role in various industries, including **telecommunications, robotics, artificial intelligence, semiconductor design, and networking**.

---

## Key Highlights of B.Tech in ECE

### Program Structure
- **Duration:** 4 years (8 semesters)
- **Mode of Study:** Full-time, **practical-oriented learning**

### Key Subjects Covered

#### Core Electronics & Circuit Design
- Analog & Digital Electronics
- Microprocessors & Microcontrollers
- VLSI Design & Embedded Systems

#### Communication & Networking
- Wireless & Optical Communication
- Satellite & Mobile Communication
- 5G, IoT, and Network Security

#### Signal Processing & AI in Electronics
- Digital Signal Processing (DSP)
- AI & Machine Learning in Embedded Systems
- Robotics & Automation
