## Program Overview

**B.Tech in Computer Science and Engineering (CSE)** is a four-year undergraduate program that focuses on the principles, theories, and applications of computing. It covers a wide range of topics, including programming, data structures, algorithms, computer networks, databases, artificial intelligence, and cybersecurity. Students gain a strong foundation in both theoretical concepts and practical skills, enabling them to develop software, design computer systems, and work with emerging technologies.

The curriculum of CSE is designed to provide hands-on experience through laboratory work, projects, and internships. Advanced topics such as cloud computing, big data analytics, blockchain, and machine learning are also introduced to keep students updated with industry trends. Additionally, soft skills like teamwork, communication, and problem-solving are emphasized to prepare students for professional roles.

Graduates of B.Tech CSE have numerous career opportunities in diverse fields, including software development, data science, cybersecurity, and IT consulting. With the rapid growth of the tech industry, CSE professionals are in high demand across various sectors such as finance, healthcare, education, and e-commerce. The degree also serves as a strong foundation for higher studies and research in computer science and related disciplines.
