# B.Tech in Computer Science and Engineering (Artificial Intelligence & Machine Learning)
## Centurion University of Technology and Management, Andhra Pradesh

---

## Program Overview

The **B.Tech in Computer Science and Engineering (Artificial Intelligence & Machine Learning) (CSE – AIML)** is a **four-year undergraduate program** designed to provide students with a strong foundation in **computer science, software engineering, and the rapidly growing field of artificial intelligence and machine learning**. 

This program integrates **advanced computing techniques, data science, deep learning, natural language processing (NLP), and AI-driven software development** to prepare students for the next generation of intelligent systems and automation technologies.

With the increasing adoption of **AI and ML across industries** such as healthcare, finance, robotics, autonomous systems, cybersecurity, and e-commerce, this program equips students with **theoretical knowledge and hands-on practical skills** to build intelligent systems and solve real-world challenges.

---

## Key Program Highlights

### Duration & Study Mode
- **Duration:** 4 years (8 semesters)
- **Mode of Study:** Full-time, with industry collaborations and internship opportunities

### Core Subjects
- Data Structures & Algorithms
- Programming in Python, Java, and C++
- Database Management Systems (DBMS)
- Artificial Intelligence & Machine Learning
- Deep Learning & Neural Networks
- Natural Language Processing (NLP)
- Computer Vision & Image Processing
- Cloud Computing & Big Data Analytics
- Cybersecurity & Blockchain
- IoT & Edge Computing

### Practical Exposure
- Hands-on training through **real-world AI/ML projects, hackathons, coding challenges, and research-based learning**
- Use of industry-standard tools such as **TensorFlow, PyTorch, OpenCV, Scikit-Learn, AWS, and Google Cloud AI**

### Industry-Relevant Curriculum
- Aligned with the latest trends in AI, ML, and deep learning technologies
- Includes **capstone projects, case studies, and internships with top tech companies**

### Interdisciplinary Learning
- Combines elements of **computer science, mathematics, statistics, and cognitive sciences**

AI/ML engineers are among the **highest-paid professionals**, with salaries ranging from **₹8–20 LPA** for freshers in top companies.

### Global Career Opportunities
Graduates can work in **tech giants, startups, research labs, and multinational companies** specializing in AI, robotics, and automation.

### Diverse Industry Applications
AI and ML are transforming **healthcare, finance, e-commerce, cybersecurity, autonomous vehicles, gaming, and more**.

### Pathway to Research & Innovation
Students can pursue **higher studies (M.Tech, MS, Ph.D.) in AI/ML, Data Science, and Robotics**, contributing to cutting-edge research.
