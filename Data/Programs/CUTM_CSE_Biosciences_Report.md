# B.Tech in Computer Science and Engineering (Biosciences)
## Centurion University of Technology and Management, Andhra Pradesh

---

## Program Overview

**B.Tech in CSE (Biosciences)** is a **four-year interdisciplinary program** that combines **computer science, bioinformatics, and life sciences** to develop advanced computational solutions for biological and medical challenges. This course integrates **biotechnology, machine learning, artificial intelligence, and data science** to analyze complex biological data and drive innovations in healthcare, genomics, and drug discovery.

With the increasing role of computational methods in **genomics, personalized medicine, disease modeling, and biotechnology**, this program prepares students to work at the intersection of **biology and computing** to develop **intelligent healthcare solutions, bioinformatics tools, and biomedical applications**.

---

## Key Highlights of B.Tech CSE (Biosciences)

### Program Structure
- **Duration:** 4 years (8 semesters)
- **Mode of Study:** Full-time, practical-oriented learning with industry collaborations

### Core Subject Areas

#### Foundations of Biosciences
- Molecular Biology & Genetics
- Biochemistry & Cell Biology
- Biotechnology & Biomedical Engineering

#### Bioinformatics & Computational Biology
- Genomics & Proteomics
- Structural Bioinformatics
- Systems Biology & Computational Drug Discovery

#### Artificial Intelligence & Data Science in Biosciences
- Machine Learning for Medical Diagnostics
- Big Data in Healthcare & Genomics
- AI-driven Drug Discovery & Predictive Analytics

#### Biomedical Engineering & Imaging
- Medical Image Processing (MRI, CT, Ultrasound)
- Neural Networks for Disease Prediction
- Biomedical Signal Processing

### Hands-on Labs & Practical Exposure
- Bioinformatics Labs with Python, R, and Biopython
- AI-based Genomic Data Analysis using TensorFlow & Scikit-learn
- Biomedical Image Processing with OpenCV and MATLAB
- Drug Discovery Simulations & Computational Biology Projects

### Industry Certifications & Tools

**Bioinformatics:**
- NCBI, BLAST, Clustal Omega, Biopython

**AI/ML in Biosciences:**
- TensorFlow, PyTorch, RapidMiner

**Computational Drug Discovery:**
- AutoDock, Schrödinger, Chemoinformatics

## Note on Credit Structure

The credit structure format used in the curriculum is represented as:
- **First number:** Theory hours per week
- **Second number:** Practical/Lab hours per week
- **Third number:** Project/Self-study hours per week

For example, **2+0+1** means 2 hours of theory, 0 hours of practical, and 1 hour of project/self-study per week.
