# B.Tech in Computer Science and Engineering (Computer Networking)
## Centurion University of Technology and Management, Andhra Pradesh

---

## Program Overview

The **B.Tech in Computer Science and Engineering (Computer Networking)** is a **four-year undergraduate program** designed to provide in-depth knowledge of **network infrastructure, cybersecurity, cloud computing, and wireless communication**. This program focuses on **designing, configuring, securing, and managing computer networks**, making students **industry-ready professionals** in networking and cybersecurity.

With the rapid growth of **cloud computing, IoT, and 5G networks**, organizations worldwide require skilled **network engineers, cybersecurity analysts, and cloud architects** to ensure smooth and secure communication. This program prepares students to work with **enterprise networks, data centers, telecommunication systems, and network security protocols**.

- Hands-on experience with **Cisco, Juniper, and Palo Alto networking devices**

- Covers **emerging network trends like AI-driven networking, cloud security, and edge computing**

### Interdisciplinary Learning
- Integration of **IoT, AI, blockchain, and machine learning with networking**
