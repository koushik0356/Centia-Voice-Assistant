# B.Tech in Computer Science and Engineering (Data Science)
**Centurion University of Technology and Management, Andhra Pradesh**

---

## Program Overview

**Program Name:** B.Tech in Computer Science and Engineering (Data Science) (CSE – DS)

**Program Type:** Four-year undergraduate program

**Program Focus:** The program focuses on the science of analyzing data to extract meaningful insights and make informed decisions. It combines computer science, artificial intelligence, machine learning, statistics, and big data analytics to equip students with the technical skills required to handle massive datasets and develop data-driven solutions.

**Context:** With the exponential growth of data in industries such as finance, healthcare, e-commerce, social media, and cybersecurity, the demand for skilled data scientists and analysts is at an all-time high. This program offers a blend of theoretical foundations and hands-on practical experience to prepare students for a successful career in data science, AI, and analytics.
