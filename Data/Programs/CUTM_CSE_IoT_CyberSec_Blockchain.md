# B.Tech in Computer Science and Engineering (IoT & Cyber Security with Blockchain Technology)
## Centurion University of Technology and Management, Andhra Pradesh

---

## Program Overview

**B.Tech in CSE (IoT & Cyber Security with Blockchain Technology)** is a **four-year interdisciplinary program** that combines **computer science, Internet of Things (IoT), cybersecurity, and blockchain technology** to develop secure, distributed digital solutions for modern enterprises and emerging technologies.

This program integrates **network security, cryptography, distributed ledger systems, IoT protocols, and secure systems architecture** to prepare students for roles in securing critical infrastructure, developing blockchain applications, and deploying secure IoT ecosystems.

With the rapid growth of connected devices, cyber threats, and decentralized technologies, this program prepares students to design and implement **secure IoT networks, blockchain-based applications, cybersecurity frameworks, and resilient digital infrastructure** across industries.

## Credit Structure Explanation

The credit structure format used in the curriculum is represented as:
- **First number:** Theory hours per week
- **Second number:** Practical/Lab hours per week
- **Third number:** Project/Self-study hours per week

For example, **2+0+1** means 2 hours of theory, 0 hours of practical, and 1 hour of project/self-study per week.
