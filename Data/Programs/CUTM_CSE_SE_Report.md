# B.Tech in Computer Science and Engineering (Software Engineering)
## Centurion University of Technology and Management, Andhra Pradesh

---

## Program Overview

**B.Tech in Computer Science and Engineering (Software Engineering) (CSE – SE)** is a **four-year undergraduate program** designed to equip students with **advanced software development, system design, and engineering principles**. This program focuses on **the entire software development lifecycle (SDLC), including requirement analysis, design, coding, testing, deployment, and maintenance**.

With the **increasing reliance on software solutions** across industries like IT, healthcare, finance, e-commerce, and gaming, there is a **growing demand for highly skilled software engineers**. The program ensures students gain **hands-on experience in programming, software architecture, agile methodologies, DevOps, cloud computing, and cybersecurity**, making them **industry-ready professionals**.
