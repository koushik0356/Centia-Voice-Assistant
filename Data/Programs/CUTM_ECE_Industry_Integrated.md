# B.Tech in Electronics and Communication Engineering (Industry Integrated)
## Centurion University of Technology and Management, Andhra Pradesh

---

## Program Overview

The **B.Tech in Electronics and Communication Engineering (Industry Integrated)** is a four-year undergraduate program designed to bridge the gap between academic learning and real-world industry applications. This course focuses on core electronics, embedded systems, communication technologies, AI, IoT, and 5G networks while incorporating hands-on training, internships, and live industry projects.

The Industry Integrated approach ensures that students gain practical exposure, certifications, and hands-on experience with cutting-edge technologies used in electronics, telecommunications, automation, and semiconductor industries.

---

## Key Highlights

### Program Duration & Mode
- **Duration:** 4 years (8 semesters)
- **Mode of Study:** Full-time, hands-on learning with industry collaborations

### Key Industry Domains Covered
- Embedded Systems & IoT
- Artificial Intelligence in Electronics
- VLSI & Semiconductor Technologies
- 5G, Wireless & Optical Communication
- Robotics & Automation
- Cybersecurity in Communication Systems

### Hands-on Labs & Practical Exposure
- VLSI & Chip Design Labs with Cadence & MATLAB
- IoT Prototyping using Raspberry Pi & Arduino
- 5G & Wireless Communication Simulations
- Robotics & Automation with AI & ML

### Industry Collaboration & Internship Programs
- Real-world projects with companies like Intel, Qualcomm, Ericsson, and Cisco
- Internships in semiconductor, telecom, and AI-driven industries
- Industry-driven capstone projects & hackathons

### Certifications & Tools Used
**Embedded Systems:**
- Keil, ARM, FPGA programming

**IoT & AI:**
- Python, TensorFlow, Edge AI in electronics

**Wireless Communication:**
- MATLAB, NS2, CST Studio
