## Program Overview

The **B.Tech in Mechanical Engineering (Automobile)** is a **four-year undergraduate program** designed to equip students with **in-depth knowledge of vehicle design, manufacturing, dynamics, and advanced automotive technologies**. The program focuses on **engine systems, electric vehicles (EVs), hybrid technologies, autonomous driving, and smart vehicle automation**.

With the global shift towards **sustainable and intelligent transportation**, this course prepares students for careers in **automobile design, research & development, vehicle testing, and emerging areas like EVs and AI-driven automotive systems**.

## Key Highlights of B.Tech in Mechanical Engineering (Automobile)

- **Duration:** 4 years (8 semesters)
- **Mode of Study:** Full-time, **industry-integrated with hands-on labs**

### Key Subjects Covered

**Core Mechanical & Automotive Engineering Concepts**

- Vehicle Dynamics & Design
- Internal Combustion Engines & Hybrid Powertrains
- Fluid Mechanics & Thermodynamics in Automobiles

**Emerging Automotive Technologies**

- Electric & Hybrid Vehicles (EVs & HEVs)
- Autonomous Vehicles & AI in Automobiles
- Smart Vehicle Automation & IoT in Cars

**Manufacturing & Safety Standards**

- Automotive CAD & CAE (SolidWorks, ANSYS, CATIA)
- Automotive Testing & Crash Analysis
- Sustainable Transportation & Emission Control

### Hands-on Labs & Practical Exposure

- Automobile Engine & Transmission Labs  
- Vehicle Dynamics Simulation & Testing  
- EV Battery & Charging System Labs  
- Autonomous Driving & AI-Driven Automotive Systems

### Industry Certifications & Tools Used

**Vehicle Design & Analysis:** AutoCAD, SolidWorks, CATIA, ANSYS  
**Automotive Simulation:** MATLAB, Simulink, AVL Cruise, ADAMS  
**Electric & Hybrid Vehicle Technology:** EV Design, Battery Management Systems

## Why Choose B.Tech in Mechanical Engineering (Automobile)?

- **Future of Mobility** – Learn about **EVs, hybrid technology, AI-driven self-driving cars, and smart transportation**.
- **High Industry Demand** – Rising demand for **automobile engineers in electric vehicles, fuel-efficient cars, and sustainable mobility**.
- **Global Career Opportunities** – Work with top **automotive brands, R&D firms, and technology-driven automobile companies**.
- **Interdisciplinary Approach** – Combines **mechanical, electronics, AI, and IoT for next-gen automotive engineering**.

## Career Path & Job Roles

Graduates of **B.Tech in Mechanical Engineering (Automobile)** can pursue careers in:

### 1. Automotive Design & Manufacturing

- Automobile Design Engineer  
- CAD/CAM Engineer for Automotive Industry  
- Vehicle Production & Quality Control Engineer

### 2. Electric & Hybrid Vehicles (EV/HEV) Development

- EV Battery Design Engineer  
- Hybrid Powertrain Engineer  
- Renewable Energy Specialist for Vehicles

### 3. Autonomous & AI-Driven Vehicles

- AI & Machine Learning Engineer for Automotive Systems  
- Autonomous Vehicle Simulation Engineer  
- IoT & Connected Car Engineer

### 4. Automotive Testing & Safety

- Vehicle Testing & Crash Analysis Engineer  
- Emission Control & Sustainability Engineer  
- Automotive Safety & Compliance Engineer

### 5. Research & Development in Automotive Engineering

- Automobile Research Scientist  
- Advanced Materials & Lightweight Vehicle Engineer  
- Aerodynamics & Fluid Dynamics Specialist
